## General style notes
- Consistent warm, natural daylight — avoid harsh flash or studio-only lighting.
- Real Kenyan settings and vehicles (matatus, pickups, sedans) rather than generic
  stock-photo cars.
- Diverse ages and genders across the student and instructor photography.
- Avoid any visible real brand logos, license plates with real numbers, or
  identifiable real people.
